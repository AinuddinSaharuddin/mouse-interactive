01 — Tilt Effects

Cards that follow your cursor

Hover over each card to see a 3D tilt effect and spotlight glow that tracks your mouse position precisely.

3D Tilt

Pure CSS transforms calculated from mouse position within each card — no library required.

Spotlight Glow

A radial gradient follows your cursor inside the card, creating a natural light source effect.

Smooth Physics

Spring-like easing on mouse leave returns the card to its resting position with a natural bounce.

Depth Layers

Each element on the card moves at a slightly different rate, creating an illusion of physical depth and weight.

Light Tracing

The glow tracks the cursor and interpolates smooth transitions using CSS custom properties updated by JavaScript.

Perspective

CSS perspective-origin combined with rotateX/Y creates a realistic 3D plane effect based on your cursor angle.
